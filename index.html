PCB Manufacturing

A printed circuit board (PCB) is a laminated sandwich structure of conductive and insulating layers. PCBs have two complementary functions. The first is to affix electronic components in designated locations on the outer layers by means of soldering.

Our Services

Single Side PCB

Single side PCB is a one layer PCB, in which all electronic components are on one side of the board and all circuits at another layer. We aim to provide the best Single sided PCBs in promised time.

Double Side PCB

A Double sided PCB is a complex entity because the Size & Efficiency are the major factors. Keep this in mind, we have trained ourselves to create the best PCBs possible.

Metal Clad PCB

Metal Clad Printed Circuits Boards (or MCPCB) are typically manufactured using either aluminium or copper as the base substrate of the PCB.
